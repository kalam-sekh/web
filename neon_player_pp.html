Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Neon Player app (herein referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by aki as a Free service.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed as a local music player. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information to external servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

2. Permissions & Data Access

To provide its core features, the Application requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Access (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_AUDIO): Used exclusively to scan your device for local audio files so you can play them within the app.
  • Audio Recording (RECORD_AUDIO): This is required solely for the Visualizer feature. It allows the app to process the audio frequency data to display the neon bars.
    Note: We do NOT record, save, or share any audio or conversations.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Used to provide playback controls (Play, Pause, Skip) in your device's notification drawer.

3. Local Data Storage

The Application saves your last played song and playback position locally on your device (using SharedPreferences) to provide a seamless "resume" experience. This data stays on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

4. Third-Party Access

Since the Application does not have internet access or third-party SDKs (like analytics or ads) in its current version, no data is shared with third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
