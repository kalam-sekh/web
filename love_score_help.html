❤️ How to Use

  1. Open the app and tap the first name field.
  2. Type your name (or any first name).
  3. Tap the second name field and enter your partner's name.
  4. Tap Calculate to reveal your love score.
  5. Read your compatibility result and share it with your partner!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the love score real?

Love Score Match is a fun entertainment app. The results are generated for amusement and are not based on scientific analysis.

Why do I get different scores for the same names?

Scores are calculated based on the names you enter. Spelling and spacing matter — make sure the names are entered the same way each time.

Does the app save my names?

No. Love Score Match does not store, collect, or transmit any personal information. Everything stays on your device.

Why is there an ad in the app?

The app is free to use and is supported by a small banner advertisement. This helps keep the app free for everyone.

The app crashed or isn't working. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you are running the latest version from the App Store, then contact us below.

🔒 Privacy

Love Score Match does not collect any personal data. No account is required. Ads are served by Google AdMob, which may use anonymous identifiers for ad personalization in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.
