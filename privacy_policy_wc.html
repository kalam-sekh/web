Privacy Policy

✓ No Personal Data Collected

Overview

World Capitals ("the App") is a reference app that displays world capitals, country flags, and educational articles. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal information.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage data, analytics, or behavioral data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Photos, contacts, or any device content

How the App Works

The app loads country and capital data from Google Firebase Realtime Database, which is hosted and managed by us solely to deliver app content. This is a one-way, read-only connection — no user data is sent to or stored in our database.

The only preference stored is your dark/light mode choice, which is saved locally on your device using Apple's standard storage and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase (by Google) to serve content. Firebase may collect minimal technical information such as network request logs as part of its standard infrastructure operation. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

No other third-party SDKs, ad networks, or analytics services are used.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for use by all ages.
