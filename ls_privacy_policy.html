Introduction

Welcome to Love Score Match ("the App"), developed by AKI ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Love Score Match, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

Love Score Match does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not store the names you enter into the app.
  • We do not access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone.
  • All name inputs are processed locally on your device and are never sent to any server.

Advertising – Google AdMob

The App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain non-personal information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Anonymous advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID)
  • App usage and interaction data for ad performance measurement
  • General location (country/region level, not precise GPS)

This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, the App will request your permission before accessing your device's advertising identifier for personalized ads. You may opt out at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Children's Privacy

Love Score Match is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data stored on our servers to protect. Ad-related data handled by Google AdMob is subject to Google's own security and privacy standards.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.
